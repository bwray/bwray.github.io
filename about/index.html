Hi there

My name is Brian Wray. I'm a full-time staff member of the Field Museum of Natural History in Chicago, a full-time student studying CS at DePaul's CDM school, and I'm a husband and father. In my free time, I like to imagine a world in which I actually have some free time.

Seriously though, I do actually have time for a few hobbies in my life. I commute to work on my bicycle, which can be a real challenge in this city. My ride is nine miles long each way, and I ride every weekday unless there is snow on the ground and/or the temperature dips below 20℉ (or -6.5℃). I am an amateur photographer, but since my son was born I have been reduced to little more than a toddler paparazzo.

I really love to cook. Cooking blends both art and science together into a delicious project that had a well-defined beginning and end. I have scorched, soured and undercooked many, many dishes, but rarely do I make the same unpallatable dish twice.
