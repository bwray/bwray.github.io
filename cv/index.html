I'm a regular participant in our exhibit's "Talk to the Scientist Hour" on weekdays from 11-12. I regularly have to try and explain molecular genetics, cell biology, evolutionary biology, biological anthropology, organismal biology and a variety of topics regarding bioinformatics and computer science to a diverse set of museum goers, ranging in age from adolescents to seniors.
